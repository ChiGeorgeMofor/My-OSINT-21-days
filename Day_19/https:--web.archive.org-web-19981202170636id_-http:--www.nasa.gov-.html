STS-88 Launch Date Set for Dec. 3, 1998

NASA Space Shuttle managers have set Thursday, Dec. 3 as the launch date for STS-88, the first shuttle mission in the assembly of the International Space Station. The night time launch, at approximately 4:00 a.m. will be a spectacular sight in the skies of the Kennedy Space Center (Full Story)
